Alessandra Young: Who is Kevin Sussmans ex-wife?

Alessandra Young is the ex-wife of Kevin Sussman, an American actor, and comedian. He played Walter on the ABC comedy-drama Ugly Betty and Stuart Bloom on the CBS sitcom The Big Bang Theory. Starting with the sixth season of The Big Bang Theory, he was promoted to a series regular.

In 2011 and 2012, he worked with The Big Bang Theory co-star John Ross Bowie to create two television comedies: The Ever After Part (FOX) and The Second Coming of Rob (CBS).

Who is Kevin Sussman’s ex-wife?

Kevin Sussman’s ex-wife is Alessandra Young. They were married in January 2006 and stayed together for 6 years before they started getting marital issues. They spilt on December 31, 2012, but filed for divorce on May 28, 2014, after two years of separation. And their legal separation was entered on May 28, 2014, in Los Angeles Superior Court.
