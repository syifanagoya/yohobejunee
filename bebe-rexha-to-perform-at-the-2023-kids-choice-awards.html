Bebe Rexha to Perform at the 2023 Kids Choice Awards Celeb Secrets

Bebe Rexha is headed to the KCAs!

The diamond-selling and GRAMMY-nominated singer/songwriter has been tapped as the headline performer for the 2023 Kids’ Choice Awards, which will air on Saturday, March 4th. The network confirmed the news in a press release earlier today (February 22), stating that Rexha will “hit the KCA stage for an epic performance of her chart-topping hit single “I’m Good (Blue).”

A celebration of fan-favorite stars across the worlds of film, television, music, sports and more, the 2023 KCAs will also feature appearances from today’s most popular stars, including Awkwafina, Lil Baby, Halle Bailey, Pete Davidson, Melissa McCarthy, MrBeast, Chris Pine, Anthony Ramos, Michelle Rodriguez, and Seth Rogen.

“I’ve always loved the Nickelodeon Kids’ Choice Awards! I can’t wait to perform and see who gets slimed,” Bebe shared in a statement.

Additionally, the star-studded award show feature appearances by Nickelodeon talent: That Girl Lay Lay (That Girl Lay Lay); and Young Dylan (Tyler Perry’s Young Dylan), who will perform his brand-new single, “I Just Wanna,” produced by Jermaine Dupri and streaming globally alongside the music video on Friday, March 3.

Last month, Nickelodeon revealed that CBS Mornings co-host and CBS Sports Analyst Nate Burleson will join Charli D’Amelio to host this year’s show, which will find the duo entering a magical surreal world featuring extreme logic-defying stunts, wild celebrity collaborations, illusions and tricks and of course — epic slimming.

This year, Jenna Ortega, Jack Harlow, GAYLE, Joji, Nicky Youre and Letitia Wright are up for their first-ever orange blimps in some of the biggest categories, while Stranger Things leads the pack with six nominations. That Girl Lay Lay, High School Musical: The Musical: The Series and The Fairly OddParents: Fairly Odder follow with four nominations each and more musical performances and celebrity guests will be announced at a later date. You can view a full list of nominees here and cast your vote at KidsChoiceAwards.com.

Don’t forget: the 2023 Kids’ Choice Awards air LIVE from the Microsoft Theater in Los Angeles on Saturday, March 4th @ 7PM ET/PT across Nickelodeon, TeenNick, Nicktoons, the Nick Jr. channel, TVLand, CMT and MTV2.
