G HERBO AND TAINA WILLIAMS TAKE THEIR KIDS TO THE PUMPKIN PATCH

It was a family affair at the pumpkin patch as G Herbo and Taina Williams took their kids, Essex and Emmy, to pick out the perfect pumpkin for carving earlier this week. The celebrity parents shared pictures from their outing with social media fans.

Essex was definitely ready for a day at the patch. The little one walked amongst the smaller pumpkins while his parents took pictures to cherish the memories. Emmy was also ready for a day of fun as she followed her older brother’s lead in exploring the pumpkin patch.

“I just love me some Essex,” one supporter said of Taina and G Herbo’s son. “He is Herb 2.0,” the fan added.

G Herbo and Taina Williams made Essex a big brother earlier this year. The celebrity parents welcomed their second child on May 17, 2022.

“Your two babies are not even a year apart,” one fan noted in reference to Essex who was born on May 27, 2021. “You got yourself Irish twins,” another social media follower shared. “That’s so cute,” the fan continued. “They will definitely have an amazing bong.”

Taina Williams has been the doting mom since learning that she would give birth to a son in 2020. The Reality TV star shared several candids during both her expectancies as a way to give fans updates on her journeys. Taina also uploaded pictures of Essex and Emmy to introduce them to the world.

Emmy’s first Instagram photo was nothing short of precious as the little one was photographed while resting in a basket. G Herbo and Taina’s daughter had a stuffed animal by her side during the photo session, which made all the shots even sweeter.

“Absolutely beautiful,” wrote one social media fan after taking in the candids. “I saw her picture on your mom’s Instagram,” another supporter told Taina. “She’s super adorable.”

In all G Herbo fathers three kids.
