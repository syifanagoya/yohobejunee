Margot Robbie Will Host Saturday Night Live Season Premiere

Suicide Squad’s Margot Robbie will host the season premiere of Saturday Night Live on October 1.

Margot Robbie portrays “Harley Quinn” in the Suicide Squad movie, which grossed over 700 million worldwide. The actress also reportedly signed on to film a spin-off movie that will focus specifically on her character.

Saturday Night Live will be entering its 42nd season and will introduce three new cast members: Alex Moffat, Melissa Villaseñor, and Mikey Day.

The musical guest for the season’s first episode will be The Weeknd, who recently announced his new album, Starboy, which will release on November 25.
